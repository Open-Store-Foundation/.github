# Open Store Foundation

The Open Store Foundation is building the future of decentralised application distribution. Our mission is to create an open, transparent, and community-driven alternative to traditional app stores, leveraging blockchain technology to ensure fairness, security, and developer freedom.

## 🌟 Project Overview

Open Store is a decentralised application store that empowers developers and users through:

- **Decentralised Architecture**: Built on blockchain technology for transparency and censorship resistance
- **Fair Revenue Sharing**: Transparent and equitable distribution of revenue between developers and platform
- **Community Governance**: Democratic decision-making processes for platform evolution
- **Multi-platform Support**: Supporting multiple platforms and devices
- **Developer Freedom**: No arbitrary restrictions or gatekeeping
